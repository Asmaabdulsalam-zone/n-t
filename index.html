About Intellectual Property in Yemen :

  • General information :

    The Republic of Yemen is located in the southern of the Arabian Peninsula in Western Asia (Sana'a is the capital city). Population in Yemen in 2019 is estimated at 29 million people. It is bordered by the Red Sea to the west, the Gulf of Aden to the south, Arabian Sea and Oman to the east and Saudi Arabia to the north.

  • Intellectual Property Authorities :
    • The Ministry of Industry and Trade is responsible for the industrial property as the trademarks, patents and industrial designs.
    • The Ministry of Culture is responsible for literary property related to copyright and related rights.
    • Ministry of Communications and Information Technology Responsible for the domain names.
  • Membership in international agreements related to intellectual property :

    Yemen is a member of the Paris Convention for the Protection of Industrial Property, the Berne Convention for the Protection of Literary and Artistic Works and the Agreement Establishing the World Intellectual Property Organization WIPO and a member of the World Trade Organization WTO.

About the trademark :

  • A trademark is a sign used to identify products or services. A trademark may consist of names, words, letters, numbers, phrase, symbol or combinations of these elements.
  • The term of protection of the trademark is ten years from the date of filing the application. The protection of the trademark shall continue as long as it has been renewed each for like period(s).
  • Yemen applies the eighth edition of the Nice Classification. Excluded from the registration are all products in class 33, alcoholic in class 32 and pork meat in class 29.
  • A separate application should be submitted for each class.
  • Priority could be claimed within 6 months from the priority date.
  • Opposition may be filed within ninety days from the date of publication in the Official Gazette.

Documents required to file a trademark application:

  • An authorization/a power of Attorney duly legalized up to the Yemen Consulate.
  • A copy of the certificate of incorporation or an extract from the commercial register that includes name, address and the objectives of corporation (no need for legalization)
  • Identical photos of the trademark in jpg format
  • List of goods or services.
  • A copy of the priority document in case of claiming priority.

About Patents :

  • A patent is an intellectual property exclusive right granted for an invention, which is a product or a process or both that provide a new solution to a technical problem
  • The term of protection of the patent is 20 years from the date of filing the application
  • Opposition may be filed within ninety days from the date of publication in the Official Gazette.

Documents required to file a patent application:

  • An authorization/a power of Attorney duly legalized up to the Yemen Consulate.
  • A copy of the certificate of incorporation or an extract from the commercial register that includes name, address and the objectives of corporation (no need for legalization)
  • Abstract, background, summary, detailed description, conclusion and claims of invention.
  • A copy of the priority document in case of claiming priority.

Industrial designs informations :

  • An industrial design constitutes the ornamental appearance or aesthetic aspect of a product
  • The term of protection of the industrial design is 10 years
  • Opposition may be filed within ninety days from the date of publication in the Official Gazette.

Documents required:

  • An authorization/a power of Attorney duly legalized up to the Yemen Consulate.
  • A copy of the certificate of incorporation or an extract from the commercial register that includes name, address and the objectives of corporation (no need for legalization)
  • The purpose of design and items to be protected (claims)
  • A copy of the priority document in case of claiming priority.

Copyright informations:

  • Copyright is Any creative literary, scientific or artistic work.
  • Law No. 15 of 2012 on the Protection of Copyright and Related Rights.
The following works are protected:

  • Written or printed works such as books, brochures, magazines, newspapers, websites, and other written materials.
  • Musical works.
  • Photography compilations.
  • Acting and theatrical works.
  • Audio and audiovisual works.
  • Drawing, engraving, decoration.
  • Stereoscopic works.
  • Computer programs & Databases.

Filing Requirements:

  • Legalised POA ( Download )
  • Copie of the art work.

Domain Names informations :

  • The Domain name is used to identify one or more IP addresses on the internet.
  • Yemen net is the competent authority responsible for the registration of domain names.
